Key Takeaways ‚Äî Week Summary
  • When choosing a data structure, ask: What operations do I need? What are their frequency and time requirements?
  • For algorithm selection: Is the problem asking for optimization (try DP or greedy)? Shortest path (BFS/Dijkstra)? Ordering (topological sort)?
  • The best problem-solving approach: understand the problem ‚Üí identify the pattern ‚Üí choose data structure ‚Üí implement ‚Üí verify with examples
  • On exams: read all questions first, start with what you know, show your work (partial credit!), and verify with small examples
  • DSA knowledge compounds ‚Äî the more problems you solve, the faster you recognize patterns in new problems

(Expected: [-1,0,1,2,-1,-4] ‚Üí [[-1,-1,2],[-1,0,1]])

Exercise 4: Maximum Product Subarray ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê Hard

Find the contiguous subarray with the largest product.

(Expected: [2,3,-2,4] ‚Üí 6)

Hint 1 - Sign Flip:
Unlike sum, product with negative number flips the sign.
Hint 2 - Track Both:
Keep track of both max AND min product ending at position i.
Hint 3 - Swap:
When num is negative, swap max and min before updating.
